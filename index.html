About Me

Hi, I'm Ashley (Ash) Dinh, a marketing professional with a creative background in visual arts and design. Based in Seattle, Washington, I recently graduated from the University of Washington with a Bachelor of Arts in Interdisciplinary Visual Arts and DXARTS, with a focus in Visual Media and Communication.

Transitioning from design to marketing, I've discovered my passion lies in connecting brands with their audiences through strategic campaigns, community outreach, and data-driven marketing initiatives. My design expertise gives me a unique advantage in creating visually compelling marketing materials that capture attention and drive engagement.

Currently working as an Outreach and Administrative Coordinator at Lotus Learning Center, I lead community outreach campaigns, conduct market research, and manage multi-channel marketing efforts. Previously, as a Marketing Coordinator Intern at 2727 Life, I designed product packaging and marketing materials that enhanced brand identity and product appeal.

With over 5 years of freelance experience creating custom illustrations and designs for local businesses, I've built strong skills in client collaboration, project management, and translating brand visions into tangible results. Now, I'm excited to bring my creative problem-solving and strategic thinking to a full-time marketing role where I can help brands grow and thrive.
