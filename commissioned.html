About My Commissioned Work

Over the past several years, I've had the privilege of creating custom artwork for clients who discovered my work through word of mouth and personal Instagram postings. Each commission tells a unique story and reflects the personal vision of the client.

My commissioned work spans multiple mediums including oil painting, screen printing, and collaborative woodworking projects. I work closely with clients to understand their vision and bring their ideas to life through thoughtful design and careful craftsmanship.

Below are some highlights from my commissioned projects, showcasing the diversity of techniques and subjects I've had the opportunity to explore.

01.
Oil
Painting

Studio Apartment at Sunset

This oil painting commission captures the warm, golden light of a sunset streaming through the windows of a studio apartment. Using traditional oil painting techniques, I created depth and atmosphere through layered brushwork and careful attention to light and shadow.

The piece evokes a sense of tranquility and urban sophistication, celebrating the unique perspective and ambiance of the client's living space during the magical hour of sunset.

Project Details
  • Medium: Oil on canvas
  • Technique: Traditional oil painting with layered brushwork
  • Subject: Penthouse studio apartment interior at sunset
  • Client Found Through: Instagram posting
02.
Wood
Speaker

Pinecone Wood-Printed Speaker

This unique commission involved collaborating on a custom wood-printed speaker design featuring pinecone motifs. The project combined functional design with natural aesthetics, creating a speaker that serves as both an audio device and a decorative art piece.

The wood printing technique highlights the organic texture and pattern of pinecones, bringing the beauty of nature into everyday technology. This commission challenged me to merge artistic vision with practical functionality.

Project Details
  • Medium: Wood printing on speaker housing
  • Technique: Custom wood printing with pinecone design
  • Function: Decorative functional speaker
  • Client Found Through: Word of mouth
03.
Screen
Prints

Custom Screen Print Designs

These screen print commissions showcase the versatility of the medium, from celebratory holiday designs to intimate nature scenes. Each print required careful planning of color layers and precise registration to achieve the final result.

Lunar New Year Flower Print

Created to celebrate Lunar New Year, this vibrant flower print captures the joy and renewal of the holiday. The layered screen printing technique brings depth and richness to the floral design, making it a perfect piece for festive decoration.

Project Details
  • Medium: Screen print on paper
  • Technique: Multi-layer screen printing
  • Theme: Lunar New Year celebration
  • Client Found Through: Personal Instagram posting

Green Forest - Couple Holding Hands

This intimate screen print depicts a couple holding hands in a lush green forest. The layered green tones create depth and atmosphere, evoking the peaceful solitude of a walk through nature together. The silhouettes of the couple add a personal, romantic element to the natural landscape.

Project Details
  • Medium: Screen print on paper
  • Technique: Layered screen printing with multiple green tones
  • Theme: Couple in nature, intimate forest scene
  • Client Found Through: Word of mouth
