The Challenge

DJ ChrisCharma needed consistent, high-quality marketing content for his music project, The Walk Club—a music movement centered around community, connection, and curated playlists for walking experiences.

The goal wasn't to build a brand from scratch, but to create fresh, engaging content each week that would keep followers invested and excited. We needed a sleek, modern design system inspired by lifestyle brands like Nike and Lululemon—clean, bold, and instantly recognizable—while allowing for creative variation week to week.

My Role

  • Marketing Designer: Created weekly social media content and promotional materials
  • Brand Designer: Developed a cohesive visual system through iterative design
  • Creative Collaborator: Worked directly with ChrisCharma to deliver fresh content that kept the community engaged

Design Goals

  • Weekly Engagement: Create fresh, compelling content each week to keep followers invested
  • Minimalist & Bold: Develop a clean, impactful visual style with strong typography
  • Consistent Yet Fresh: Maintain brand cohesion while introducing new designs weekly
  • Community-Focused: Reflect the movement's emphasis on connection and inclusivity

Process

Iterative Design & Collaboration

Phase 1: Brand Discovery & Direction

Over 8 weeks, I collaborated closely with ChrisCharma to understand The Walk Club's vision and aesthetic direction. We identified key visual principles that would guide the weekly content:

Visual Principles
  • Clean & Minimalist
  • Bold Typography
  • Community-Driven Imagery
  • Movement-Focused
  • Music as Culture
Design Inspiration
  • Nike's bold simplicity
  • Lululemon's lifestyle branding
  • Vinyl record aesthetics
  • Urban street culture

Phase 2: Playlist Branding System

The core of The Walk Club's content was monthly playlists. I developed a vinyl record-inspired design system with color variations to keep content fresh:

Cream variant with bold typography and clean layout

Yellow variant for visual variety while maintaining consistency

Phase 3: Tracklist Variations

To deepen engagement, I created detailed tracklist designs that showcased individual songs. Through iteration each week, we refined multiple design concepts:

Version 1: Text-focused with strong hierarchy

Version 2: Image-driven with lifestyle photography

Phase 4: Extended Playlist Series

Each week brought new content opportunities. I expanded the design system to accommodate new playlists with themed content:

Playlist 002: "The After Walk"

Tracklist variation with shoes imagery

Alternative tracklist layout

Phase 5: Brand Messaging & Community Content

Beyond playlists, I developed weekly content that communicated the movement's philosophy and kept followers engaged:

"We walk together. We grow together. That's the movement."

"It's not about how far you go. It's about how you feel when you get there."

Design Principles Applied
  • Consistency with Variation: Every design used the same typography system and color palette, but introduced new layouts and imagery weekly
  • Fresh Content: Created diverse post types to keep followers engaged and excited about new content
  • Scalability: Designed for Instagram, with versions optimized for stories, posts, and carousel content
  • Community Focus: Incorporated lifestyle imagery that reflected the diverse, inclusive nature of the movement

Collaborative Workflow

Working directly with ChrisCharma, we established a weekly rhythm for delivering fresh content:

  1. Content Planning: ChrisCharma provided playlist themes and messaging direction
  2. Design Exploration: I created 2-3 design variations for each week's content
  3. Quick Feedback: Fast iteration cycles via Figma to meet weekly posting schedule
  4. Asset Delivery: Export optimized files for Instagram posts and stories
  5. Strategic Posting: Coordinated timing to maximize follower engagement
